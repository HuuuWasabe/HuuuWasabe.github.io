Who am I?

Howdy! My name is Nil Jedidiah G. San Gabriel. I am a graduate of Computer Engineering from the Technological Institute of the Philippines, specializing in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science, and I am eager to begin my carrer in Software Engineering.

Through my academic coursework, I developed a solid foundation in machine learning, deep learning, data science, and software development. I participated in the ASEAN Data Science Explorers 2023 and completed Cisco Networking Courses and the AWS Cloud Practitioner certification, which strengthened my understanding of computer networks and cloud computing. For my capstone project, “Design of Activity Recognition System for a Weight Training Performance Assessment Using Deep Learning,” I was responsible for training our deep learning model, developing a module for exercise assessment, and designing a user interface for the device. I also maintain a personal Homelab, where I experiment with deploying local web services and improving system configurations.
